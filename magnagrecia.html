Selinunte North Entrace Museum

Archeology and Scenography

The Magna Grecia workshop, part of the Master in Architecture and Archaeology at the Accademia Adrianea, took place from June 11 to 17, 2023, in the archaeological parks of Segesta, Selinunte, and on the island of Mozia in Sicily. The project aimed at creating exhibition spaces integrated with archaeological remains, inspired by the script of the musical "Jesus Christ Superstar." Specifically, an exhibition route was developed at the Porta Nord of the Selinunte Archaeological Park, featuring an outdoor exhibition space and an underground area connected by a new pathway.
