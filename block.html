BlockWonder Ferry

The BlockWonder Ferry project reimagines decks 10 and 11 of the Olympia ferry by transforming them into dynamic, Lego-themed spaces that provide a range of activities for passengers of all ages. Previously used as standard transit areas, these decks are now designed to be interactive environments where creativity and play take center stage.
Passengers can engage in building giant Lego structures, participate in collaborative art projects, or enjoy role-playing games and creative workshops. There are dedicated zones for younger children, teenagers, and adults, allowing everyone to enjoy activities tailored to their interests. The design promotes multigenerational interaction, creating opportunities for families and friends to bond through shared experiences.
By incorporating the familiar and versatile Lego theme, the project encourages passengers to take an active role in their journey, turning travel time into a memorable adventure. The spaces provide not only entertainment but also a chance for relaxation and socialization, making the journey itself an enjoyable part of the overall experience.
